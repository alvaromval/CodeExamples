 Control list:
The Contro list is an instrument used for enumerating characteristics, competences, features, behaviours, attributes or series of acts which presence
or absence must be checked.
With this instrument, the evaluator can check if the features, characteristics or attributes are present or not in the assessed subject.
Control lists are frequently used when is needed to obtanin information about:
a) Proccesses and tasks
b) Results of and action, of a task or a learning activity in which can be checked if certain features/characteristics have been fulfilled. 
c) Performance features.
